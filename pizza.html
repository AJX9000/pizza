The Wonders of Sour Fruit on Savory Cheese

My Favorite Toppings

    Pineapple
    Extra Cheese
    Meatballs
    Chicken
    Pepperoni

In case you couldn't tell from the main heading of this page, my favorite topping is pineapple. It's honestly wondrous that pizza's biggest contradiction - a sweet and particularly sour fruit - would actually be one of the biggest compliments to the salty and savory dish. At first, I was skeptical of the taste, but thanks to good peer pressure, I take pineapple on pizza at any opportunity.

Pizzas I Like

    Pepperoni Pizza
    Meatball and Sausage Pizza

As if pineapple wasn't enough, ham (sometimes called Canadian bacon) ends up being the final ingredient to my favorite pizza. I usually don't care about ham on pizza, though the tropical fruit of sin says otherwise. Also, Hawaiian pizza is not Hawaiian; it was invented by a Canadian immigrant named Sam Panopoulos. Sadly, he passed away in 2017, but he left quite a legacy, alright. Thanks, internet.
